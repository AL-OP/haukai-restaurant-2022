About Us

The Haukai Restaurant has created a menu with the fusion of Māori and Pākeha kai, in the hopes of letting guests experience Te Ao Māori through our delicious meals.

Come visit us in the beautiful Bay of Islands and leave with a full stomach and a new appreciation for the world around us.
